## Binds

```md
bind [key] sm_saveloc
bind [key] sm_tele
```

for example:

```md
bind mouse1 sm_saveloc
bind mouse2 sm_tele
```

Turn off surftimer: `!surftimer`

Teleport to location: `sm_loadloc #[loc]`

(`sm_` for in console `!` for in chat)
